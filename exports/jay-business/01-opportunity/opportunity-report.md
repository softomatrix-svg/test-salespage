## AI Workflow Automation Templates

**Why this wins:**
- ✅ **Urgency:** Businesses need cost savings NOW in uncertain economy
- ✅ **Speed to revenue:** Can build MVP in 1 week, sell immediately
- ✅ **Competition:** Fragmented market, no dominant player for "done-for-you" templates

**Core Problem:** They waste 10-20 hours/week on manual tasks they could automate, but feel overwhelmed by the tools and don't have time to learn.

**Unique Angle:** "Copy-paste automation templates — no learning curve, working systems in 5 minutes"
