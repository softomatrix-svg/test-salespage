# STAGE 2 — PRODUCT ARCHITECTURE

## Business Blueprint: AI Workflow Automation Templates

---

## Core Offer

A collection of 10 "copy-paste" workflow automation templates for solopreneurs and small business owners. Each template includes:
- Pre-built automation (Zapier/Make ready)
- Setup video (2-3 minutes)
- Customization guide
- Troubleshooting checklist

---

## Unique Positioning

**"The only automation templates designed for non-techies — no learning curve, no coding, working systems in 5 minutes"**

**Differentiation:** Competitors sell courses (learn to build). We sell working systems (copy and use).

---
