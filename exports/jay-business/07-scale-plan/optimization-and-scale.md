## 3 Optimization Levers

### Lever 1: Sales Page Conversion

| Metric | Target | If Below Target |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Page load speed | <3 seconds | Compress images, minify CSS |
| Bounce rate | <40% | Strengthen headline/hook |
| Time on page | >2 minutes | Add video, improve flow |
| CTA click rate | >5% | Test button color/copy |
| Conversion rate | >2% | A/B test price, guarantee, offer stack |

**Quick Wins:**
- Add urgency timer (if doing a launch)
- Test "Get Instant Access" vs "Buy Now" vs "Start Automating"
- Add social proof section (testimonials when available)
- Exit-intent popup with discount or lead magnet

---

### Lever 2: Traffic Quality & Cost

| Channel | Target CPA | Optimization |
|---------|-----------|--------------|
| LinkedIn organic | $0 | Post frequency, engagement hooks |
| Email list | $0 | Segment by engagement, resend to non-openers |
| Paid social | <$50 | Kill underperforming ads, scale winners |
| Retargeting | <$30 | Frequency capping, creative refresh |

**Quick Wins:**
- Retarget website visitors who spent >30 seconds but didn't buy
- Create lookalike audiences from buyers (when you have 50+ sales)
- Test different ad angles (problem-focused vs. outcome-focused)
