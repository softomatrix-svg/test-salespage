## VIDEO 4.2: TROUBLESHOOTING & EDGE CASES (12 min)

### SCRIPT

"Even with the frameworks, you'll hit roadblocks. Here's how to handle them.

**Problem: AI Won't Follow Instructions**

Solution: Break into smaller steps
Instead of one complex prompt, use 3 simple ones.

**Problem: Output Is Too Generic**

Solution: Add more constraints
Use Framework 2 to force uniqueness.

**Problem: Wrong Tone**

Solution: Give specific examples
"Write like this: [example] not like this: [example]"

**Problem: AI Makes Things Up**

Solution: Add fact constraints
"Only use information I provide. Use [PLACEHOLDER] for missing info."

**Problem: Repetitive Content**

Solution: Explicit constraint
"Do not repeat the same point in different words."

**Problem: Too Long/Wordy**

Solution: Specific length constraint
"Maximum 150 words. Remove filler."

**Problem: Inconsistent Results**

Solution: Save successful templates
Reuse what works. Don't reinvent.

**Edge Case: Highly Technical Topics**

Solution: Provide source material
Give AI articles, data, or briefs to work from.

**Edge Case: Creative/Brand Voice**

Solution: Train with examples
Give AI 3-5 examples of your voice first.

**Edge Case: Controversial/Sensitive Topics**

Solution: Add guardrails
"Be respectful. Avoid [specific language]. Focus on [positive angle]."

[Show before/after examples for each problem]

**The Reset Button**

When nothing works, start fresh:
"Let's start over. Forget previous instructions. New task: [simple, clear prompt]"

Sometimes a clean slate is the fastest fix."
