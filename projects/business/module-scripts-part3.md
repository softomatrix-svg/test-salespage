## VIDEO 5.1: BUILDING YOUR PROMPT WORKFLOW (10 min)

### SCRIPT

"Knowledge without action is useless. Let's build your implementation system.

**The 5-Step Workflow**

Step 1: Identify Your Top 3 Tasks
What do you create most often?
- Emails?
- Social posts?
- Sales pages?
- Content?

Pick the 3 that take the most time.

Step 2: Create Templates for Each
Using the frameworks, create 3-5 templates for each task type.

Example: Email templates
- Welcome email template
- Sales email template
- Nurture email template

Save these in a Google Doc or Notion.

Step 3: Customize for Specific Uses
When you need to create something:
1. Pull up the template
2. Fill in the brackets
3. Run the prompt
4. Refine if needed

Step 4: Save What Works
When you get great output:
1. Save the final prompt
2. Note what worked
3. Add to your library

Step 5: Iterate and Improve
Monthly review:
- What's working best?
- What needs updating?
- What new templates do I need?

**Your Prompt Library Structure**

Create folders/categories:
- Email Marketing
- Social Media
- Copywriting
- Content
- SEO
- Ads

Within each: 3-5 proven templates.

**Time Investment**

Initial setup: 2-3 hours
Daily use: 10-15 minutes
Monthly maintenance: 30 minutes

Time saved: 5-10 hours per week

ROI: Massive"

## VIDEO 5.2: MEASURING YOUR IMPROVEMENT (10 min)

### SCRIPT

"How do you know if this is working? You measure.

**What to Measure**

**Time Metrics**
- Time to create content (before vs after)
- Time to get from idea to publish
- Time saved per week

Track this for 2 weeks. You'll see dramatic improvement.

**Quality Metrics**
- Engagement rates (likes, comments, shares)
- Click-through rates
- Conversion rates
- Feedback from audience/clients

Better prompts = better content = better results.

**Consistency Metrics**
- Content published per week/month
- Adherence to content calendar
- Ability to hit deadlines

The frameworks make consistency possible.

**The 30-Day Challenge**

For the next 30 days:
1. Use the frameworks for everything
2. Track time spent
3. Track content output
4. Note quality improvements
5. Document wins

At the end of 30 days, you'll have:
- Proof the system works
- A library of proven templates
- Confidence in your AI skills
- Measurable time savings

**Baseline Measurement**

Before you start, document:
- How long does [TASK] currently take?
- How satisfied are you with current output?
- How consistent are you with publishing?

Compare after 30 days.

**The Improvement Dashboard**

Create a simple tracker:

Week | Task | Time Before | Time After | Quality | Notes
1 | Email | 2 hours | 30 min | Better | Used Framework 1
2 | Blog | 6 hours | 90 min | Better | Used Framework 3
...

Simple but powerful."
