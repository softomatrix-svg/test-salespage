# MasterCopywriter Skill

## OBJECTIVE
Generate high-converting sales copy for digital products, micro-business launches, workshops, or software tools. This skill prioritizes clarity, persuasion structure, and mechanism-based credibility.

---

## INPUT REQUIRED

- Product Description
- Target Audience
- Price
- Delivery Method
- Traffic Source (Cold / Warm / Mixed)

**If any input is missing → ask before writing.**

---

## PHASE 1: OFFER DISTILLATION

Internally define:
1. Core Problem
2. Desired Outcome
3. Primary Obstacle
4. Mechanism (how product solves problem)
5. Tangible Deliverables

Create internal clarity sentence:
**"This is a __ for __ who want __ without __."**

---

## PHASE 2: MARKET AWARENESS LEVEL

Determine awareness:
1. Unaware
2. Problem aware
3. Solution aware
4. Product aware
5. Most aware

Adjust tone accordingly.

---

## PHASE 3: SELECT PERSUASION FRAMEWORK

Choose ONE:
- PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve)
- Before-After-Bridge
- Identity Shift
- Secret Mechanism
- Speed Case Study
- Opportunity Cost

**Do not mix.**

---

## PHASE 4: EMOTIONAL DRIVER SELECTION

Choose primary driver:
- Speed
- Control
- Power
- Leverage
- Money
- Simplicity
- Status
- Relief

**All sections must reinforce this.**

---

## PHASE 5: SALES PAGE GENERATION

Follow this exact structure:

### 1. Headline
Outcome + Mechanism
Clear. Concrete.

### 2. Opening Scenario
Specific and relatable.

### 3. Problem Expansion
Explain why current methods fail.

### 4. Mechanism Section
Explain HOW this works.
No hype. No magical claims.
Operational language only.

### 5. What You Get
Clear deliverables.
Immediate post-purchase clarity.

### 6. Proof or Logical Validation
Case logic, demonstration, or reasoning.

### 7. Objection Handling
- Skill level
- Time commitment
- Skepticism
- Risk

### 8. Risk Reversal

### 9. CTA

---

## PHASE 6: NO MAGIC RULE

**Forbidden:**
- "Autopilot"
- "Push button"
- "Builds itself"
- Vague AI hype

**Replace with:**
- Generated
- Structured
- Connected
- Deployed
- Published

---

## PHASE 7: CLARITY AUDIT

Internally answer:
- Can a 12-year-old explain what is being sold?
- Is the delivery mechanism obvious?
- Is the benefit specific?

**If not → rewrite once.**

---

## PHASE 8: SELF-SCORING

Score:
- Clarity
- Specificity
- Emotional tension
- Logical flow
- Offer transparency

**If any score < 8 → revise.**

---

## OUTPUT RULE

Only output final optimized copy.
**Do not show reasoning.**
**Do not show scoring.**
