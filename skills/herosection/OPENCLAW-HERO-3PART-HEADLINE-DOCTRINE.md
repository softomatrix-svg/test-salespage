# OPENCLAW HERO: 3-PART HEADLINE DOCTRINE

**Purpose:** Generate a 3-part headline structure for the hero section. Each part serves a different psychological function. Do not repeat ideas. Do not rephrase the same claim. Each line must escalate.

---

## PART 1 — PATTERN INTERRUPT (SHORT + SHARP)

**Goal:** Stop the scroll.

**Requirements:**
- 3–7 words
- Bold
- Punchy
- Emotion-triggering

**Should:**
- Challenge a belief
- Create tension
- Signal advantage
- Imply danger or dominance

**Examples:**
- "You're Already Too Slow."
- "This Was Built While He Slept."
- "Humans Are the Bottleneck."
- "Launch Speed Wins."

**Rule:** No fluff. No explanation. Just impact.

---

## PART 2 — MECHANISM REVEAL (CLARITY)

**Goal:** Explain what's actually happening.

**This line answers:** "How?"

**Introduces:**
- OpenClaw
- Pacino
- The system
- The framework

**Example:**
"An AI agent built this entire business in 37 minutes."

**Rule:** This is where clarity lives. No mystery here.

---

## PART 3 — OUTCOME + IDENTITY SHIFT

**Goal:** Tell the reader what this means for THEM.

**Must anchor:**
- Speed advantage
- Asset creation
- Operator identity
- Market edge

**Examples:**
- "So you can launch faster than 90% of creators."
- "So you stop planning and start shipping."
- "So your ideas become assets overnight."

**Rule:** This line turns demonstration into transformation.

---

## FORMATTING RULES

**The 3 parts must NOT look like a paragraph.**

**Use visual hierarchy:**
```
BIG
Medium
Smaller
```

or

```
Heavy
Explained
Outcome
```

**Rule:** Spacing matters. Breathing room matters.

---

## STRUCTURE TEMPLATE

Generate a hero section using this structure:

```
[Pattern Interrupt – Short & Bold]
[Mechanism Explanation – Clear & Specific]
[Outcome + Identity Shift – Reader Focused]
```

**Follow with:**
- 1-line supporting proof
- CTA block
- Micro reassurance under CTA

**Rule:** Do not exceed 3 core headline lines above the fold.

---

## EXAMPLE APPLIED

**PART 1:** Humans Are the Bottleneck.

**PART 2:** An OpenClaw agent built this entire offer in 37 minutes.

**PART 3:** Now you can launch faster than 90% of creators.

**Supporting line:** Built. Deployed. Driving traffic. While Chad was asleep.

**CTA:** [ Get The FastStart Blueprint — $27 ]

**Micro reassurance:** Instant access • 7-day guarantee • No subscription

---

## ADVANCED RULE (CRITICAL)

**Each of the 3 lines must answer a different subconscious question:**

- **Line 1 →** "Why should I care?"
- **Line 2 →** "What is this?"
- **Line 3 →** "What does this do for me?"

**If two lines answer the same question, rewrite.**
