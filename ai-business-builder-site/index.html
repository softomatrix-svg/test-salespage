From “I Want an Online Business” to Launch, Step by Step

Week 1 — Clarity & Direction

Map your skills, interests, and constraints. Use AI workflows to generate and compare 3–5 directions, then pick one clear path: who you serve, what problem you help with, and what outcome you focus on.

Week 2 — Offer Design

Turn your direction into a simple, testable offer. Define what they get, how it’s delivered, and what it costs. Use templates and AI assistance to explore variations and choose one you can stand behind.

Week 3 — Positioning & Messaging

Craft a clear one-liner, elevator pitch, and offer description. Generate multiple versions with AI, then refine so it sounds like you and makes sense to your buyer.

Week 4 — Pages & Emails

Use guided prompts to generate a simple offer page and basic emails or DMs to start conversations. Edit with help so it feels honest and concrete, not like generic copy.

Week 5 — Systems & Automation

Set up a minimal stack: email list, calendar/booking, simple tracking. Add 1–2 light automations like a welcome email and booking confirmations.

Week 6 — Launch & Learn

Choose a small, realistic launch: emails, DMs, posts. Use the system to plan and write them. Track what happens and adjust your offer, message, or audience based on real feedback.
